Login Options:

  1. Admin Login
  2. Faculty Login
  3. Student Login
  4. Login via OTP

Admin:

  • The admin can add students, faculty, and courses. When adding students and faculty, it can accept photos.
  • The admin can perform operations such as delete, update, and view on students, faculty, and courses.
  • The admin can update their passwords and also map courses to faculty. To map a course, the admin must first add the course.

Faculty:

  • Faculty members can change their passwords, view their courses, and upload course content.
  • When uploading course content, they can provide web links and images.

Student:

  • Students can change their passwords and view their courses along with the course content.
